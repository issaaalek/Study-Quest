# Study Quest

Study Quest is a gamified productivity app prototype designed to help students stay focused and complete academic goals.

## Problem
Many students struggle to stay focused while studying due to digital distractions and lack of motivation, with many studies showing shocking statistics.

## Solution
Study Quest turns studying into a structured quest where students set goals, complete tasks, track progress, earn rewards, and has a focus mode.

## Features
- Goal-based study planning
- Progress tracking and levels
- Gamified rewards
- Focus mode with ambient environments
- Flexible goal resetting
